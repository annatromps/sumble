How to Play
The Goal
Use the six number tiles to reach the target using +, −, ×, ÷.
You don't have to use all the numbers, and you can use each operator as many times as you like.
The Numbers
●Big numbers: one of 25, 50, 75, 100
●Small numbers: 1 to 10
🎯Target: any number 101-999
The Rules
1.Tap a number tile, then an operator, then another number. You can chain multiple operators, e.g. 25 + 3 × 8 is evaluated with BODMAS.
2.Results of each step become new tiles you can use in the next step.
3.Division must be exact, no fractions.
4.All results must be positive.
5.Hit = to apply a step. Hit Submit when you're done.
Modes
⏱Countdown: 30 seconds, then auto-submit. Score is based on speed and accuracy.
∞Free: No timer. Just solve it.
