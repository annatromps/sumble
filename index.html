Daily Puzzle

NUMBLE

Streak 0
Target
—
Time 30
Working
Select a number, then an operator, then another number
How to Play
The Goal
Use the six number tiles to reach the target using +, −, ×, ÷.
You don't have to use all the numbers.
The Numbers
🟡Big numbers — one of 25, 50, 75, 100
🔵Small numbers — 1 through 10
🎯Target — any number 101–999
The Rules
1.Tap a number tile, then an operator (+−×÷), then another number tile, then = to apply the step.
2.Results of each step become new tiles you can use in the next step.
3.Division must be exact — no fractions.
4.All intermediate results must be positive.
5.Hit Submit when you're done — or when it auto-submits on an exact match.
The Timer
You have 30 seconds. The timer keeps going into overtime — there's no hard stop. A faster exact solution scores better on the share card.
Scoring
🎯Exact — you hit the target
🔥Very close — within 5
👍Close — within 10
💡Not quite — more than 10 away
